We think that bumping into somebody by pure chance doesn't happen often enough. We've all enjoyed chance encounters. Friends, colleagues, even future husbands . But how often have we been sitting next to somebody else who had that same potential - and we simply didn't realise? Too often, we think, and that's why we're building ambient.

See Who's Around You

ambient fuses the digital and the real world by providing you with useful context about the people who are around you right now. Think of it as seeing little information bubbles painted above peoples' heads.

Discover Unknown Connections

See in what unknown and unexpected ways you are connected with the people around you. Do you like the same music that this person over there is listening to right now? Are you reading the same book? Do you have any common friends? Do you speak the same language? And much, much more…

Control What You Share

We take privacy extremely seriously. Your information is only shared with the people who are in the same location at the same time. We never, ever reveal your exact location. Moreover, you are in complete control over what you want to share, when you want to share it and with whom.

Catch Up at Your Convenience

We know it's not always a good time for a bit of chitchat. That's why we let you see - whenever it suits you - people you've almost bumped into over the course of the day. Just to make sure you don't miss out on the opportunity to meet someone interesting.

Be the First one to know

We're working hard to flood this planet with serendipity but we're not quite there yet. We'll make sure, though, that you'll be the first one to one when we're ready to launch.
